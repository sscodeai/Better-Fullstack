Service choices add provider clients, environment variables, examples, or integration scaffolds around the core app.

## AI

TypeScript AI choices include Vercel AI SDK, Mastra, VoltAgent, LangGraph, OpenAI Agents SDK, Google ADK, ModelFusion, LangChain, LlamaIndex, TanStack AI, AI CLI, or none.

Python has a separate multi-select AI category with LangChain, LlamaIndex, OpenAI SDK, Anthropic SDK, LangGraph, CrewAI, Haystack, or none.

## Product services

Common service sections include:

- Email: React Email, Resend, Nodemailer, Postmark, SendGrid, AWS SES, Mailgun, Plunk, or none.
- Realtime: Socket.IO, PartyKit, Ably, Pusher, Liveblocks, Yjs, or none.
- Jobs: BullMQ, Trigger.dev, Inngest, Temporal, or none.
- CMS: Payload, Sanity, Strapi, TinaCMS, Directus, or none.
- Analytics: Plausible, Umami, or none.
- Feature flags: GrowthBook, PostHog, LaunchDarkly, Flagsmith, Unleash, or none.
